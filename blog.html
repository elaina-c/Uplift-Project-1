Meet Elaine: From Curiosity to Code

As a child, I had no idea what coding was or what it even meant. But every time I saw people using computers or laptops, I felt a mix of curiosity and envy. I told myself, "One day, I want to be someone who knows how to use a computer." That spark of curiosity became the beginning of my journey into the world of coding.

About Myself

When I started in the ICT strand, I didn’t really know what I wanted for the future. But I was happy because I could use a computer for two hours every school day. I really enjoyed it.

My Background

I actually finished my work immersion training at Bicutan Med as a finance assistant. Even though it wasn’t directly related to coding, I was still able to use a computer to compute budgets and do other tasks. It also gave me a chance to experience working in an office environment.

How I Got Into Coding

I became interested in web development when I realized how websites are built and how powerful coding can be. At first, I didn’t know where to start, but I got motivated through school and some online videos. Later on, I joined Uplift Code Camp, which really helped me understand the basics of HTML, CSS, and how websites work. That’s when I truly started to enjoy coding and saw it as something I want to keep learning.

What I Enjoy About Coding

My favorite part of coding is designing websites and seeing the results live on the screen—it feels exciting to bring ideas to life! Right now, I really enjoy using HTML and CSS to build and style pages. I'm also starting to explore JavaScript to make websites more interactive.

Other Things I Love

Outside of coding, I enjoy music, art, reading, and watching anime. I also love coffee—it's my go-to boost during study sessions! In my free time, I like playing games to relax and have fun.

My Goals

Right now, I'm continuing my training at Uplift Code Camp, learning more about web development each day. In the short term, I want to become a skilled front-end developer and work with a team where I can grow and learn more. In the long run, I dream of building my own apps and being part of something that makes a difference through technology.

Closing

Always remember to appreciate your friends and the people around you. Build positive connections, support one another, and most importantly—do everything with love and purpose.
